Tony Fery is a great agent on our team who mainly
focuses on the buyer side of sales. Today we wanted to talk to him a little bit
about what he likes about his job, our team, and our clients.

Tony comes from a great team culture in his previous career. He found that same team culture here, where we thrive together and push one another to the top. Tony says it’s a “competitive yet fun environment” and “a great opportunity to help someone through the biggest transaction of their lives.” He likes the education aspect of the home buying process because it really helps to take some of the fear and anxiety out of purchasing a home.

Tony’s favorite part about the job are the relationships that form out of business transactions. Whether it’s working with our team or with our buyers, it’s ultimately the relationships he builds that excite him the most.

“
It’s important to work
with an agent like Tony.

”


There is a lot of work that goes on behind the scenes in a home transaction. The transaction doesn’t stop when you accept an offer, either. Tony likes to think of that as the “engagement” while the actual day where escrow closes is the “wedding.”

Real estate is a great industry. Even though the market does change and things open up and shift, it’s very important to work with a motivated agent like Tony.
